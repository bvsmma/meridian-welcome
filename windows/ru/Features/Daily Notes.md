# Daily Notes

Press `Ctrl+D` to open or create today's note instantly.

Meridian creates a note named after today's date (e.g. `2026-05-22.md`) inside your `Daily/` folder.

---

## What to put in a daily note

**Morning capture** — write what you're working on. Dump everything on your mind.

**Running log** — add throughout the day as things happen, decisions get made, ideas arrive.

**Evening review** — what got done? What didn't? What's tomorrow's priority?

---

## Connecting daily notes to your vault

```markdown
## 2026-05-22

Working on [[Project Planning]] today.
Had an idea — see [[Canvas]].
Read something interesting in [[Book Notes]].
```

Your [[Graph View]] then shows daily notes as connectors between your other notes.

---

## Date formats

Change the format in **Settings → Files & Saving → Daily note date format**:

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| YYYY-MM-DD | 2026-05-22 |
| DD-MM-YYYY | 22-05-2026 |
| DD.MM.YYYY | 22.05.2026 |

---

See [[Daily/2026-05-22]] for a real example.

> [!TIP]
> The Calendar panel (calendar icon in sidebar) shows all your daily notes on a calendar. Click any date to open that day's note.
