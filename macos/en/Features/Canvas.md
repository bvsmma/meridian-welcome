# Canvas

Canvas is an infinite spatial workspace. Place notes, images, and text cards anywhere on a freeform board — then connect them with lines.

Open [[Meridian Overview.canvas]] to see a pre-built example.

---

## Opening a canvas

1. Click the canvas icon in the bottom toolbar of the sidebar
2. Any file ending in `.canvas` opens as a canvas

---

## What you can do

**Add a card** — double-click anywhere on the empty canvas to create a text card.

**Embed a note** — drag a `.md` file from the sidebar onto the canvas. The card shows the note's content live.

**Draw connections** — hover the edge of a card to see connection handles. Drag from a handle to another card.

**Move freely** — drag cards anywhere. Scroll to zoom. Click and drag the background to pan.

---

## When to use canvas

- **Mind maps** — brainstorming a topic from all angles
- **Project boards** — cards for tasks, arranged by status
- **Research layouts** — placing sources spatially around a central question
- **System diagrams** — mapping how components connect
